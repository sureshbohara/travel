Mountain Biking in Nepal

Nepal provides an endless chain of mountain bike trails, whether you’re an adrenaline junkie or just want to enjoy the ride. Prior to a few years ago, the main reasons people traveled to Nepal were to trek, climb, raft, or observe tigers in the wild. But now that mountain biking has become so popular, it’s only becoming greater!

Some of the finest bike tracks you’ll ever experience can be found on professional mountain biking excursions created and led by Ace the Himalaya. We can design specialized programs for cyclists of all skill levels. Similarly, some of our most preferred biking trails are Annapurna Circuit Biking and Upper Mustang Biking. Vehicle support is offered whenever needed to ensure that your journey is safe and pleasurable.
